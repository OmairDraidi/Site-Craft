## 2. Visual Identity

### 2.1 Logo System

#### Primary Logo
**"SiteCraft"** with geometric **S icon**

**Logo Concept:**
- Geometric **S** icon representing structure, building, and craftsmanship
- Modern, bold wordmark
- Perfect balance between sophistication and simplicity
- Icon can stand alone as brand mark

#### Logo Variations

**1. Horizontal Logo** (Primary)
- Full wordmark "SiteCraft" + S icon
- Usage: Website header, marketing materials, presentations
- Color: Gold on Black / Black on Gold / White on Dark

**2. Icon-Only** (Secondary)
- Geometric S icon alone
- Usage: App icons, favicons, social media avatars, loading screens
- Minimum size: 32x32px

**3. Light Version**
- Gold (#F6C453) logo on dark backgrounds
- Primary usage for dark theme interfaces

**4. Dark Version**
- Black (#0A0A0A) logo on light backgrounds
- Usage for light theme interfaces, print materials

**5. Monochrome**
- Single color (white or black) for special applications
- Usage: Watermarks, embossing, minimal contexts

#### Logo Usage Guidelines

**Minimum Sizes:**
- Horizontal logo: 140px width (digital), 35mm (print)
- Icon-only: 32px (digital), 8mm (print)

**Clear Space:**
- Minimum 24px around logo (equivalent to height of "S" icon)

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Rotate, skew, or distort the logo
- ‚ùå Change colors outside approved palette
- ‚ùå Add effects (drop shadows, gradients, glows)
- ‚ùå Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- ‚ùå Recreate or redraw the logo
- ‚ùå Use unapproved color combinations

## 3. Brand Voice & Tone

### 3.1 Voice Characteristics

**Professional & Reliable**
- Use confident, authoritative language
- Demonstrate expertise and credibility
- Maintain premium positioning

**Modern & Clear**
- Use simple, direct language
- Avoid unnecessary complexity
- Focus on clarity and precision

**Empowering & Confident**
- Focus on user success and achievement
- Use action-oriented language
- Celebrate excellence

**Premium & Sophisticated**
- Maintain elevated tone
- Use refined vocabulary
- Avoid casual or overly friendly language

### 3.2 Tone by Context

**Marketing & Sales:**
- Confident and aspirational
- Focus on premium value and excellence
- Use powerful, impactful language
- Example: *"Craft enterprise-grade websites with unparalleled speed and sophistication"*

**Product UI:**
- Clear and professional
- Efficient and precise
- Confident guidance
- Example: *"Select your premium template"*

**Documentation:**
- Professional and thorough
- Clear and structured
- Authoritative and helpful
- Example: *"Configure your custom domain with enterprise-grade security"*

**Support:**
- Professional and solution-focused
- Efficient and knowledgeable
- Respectful and helpful
- Example: *"Our team will resolve this immediately"*

### 3.3 Writing Guidelines

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Use confident, professional language
- ‚úÖ Focus on premium value and quality
- ‚úÖ Be clear and concise
- ‚úÖ Use power words (craft, premium, excellence, sophisticated)
- ‚úÖ Emphasize speed and efficiency

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Use overly casual language
- ‚ùå Overuse exclamation marks
- ‚ùå Make exaggerated claims
- ‚ùå Use cheap or discount-focused messaging
- ‚ùå Compromise on professionalism
